Employee Handbook — Quinine AI (Excerpt)

1. Working Hours
   Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
   Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval.
   Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week.

2. Time Off
   - Annual leave: 20 days per year
   - Sick leave: 10 days per year
   - Parental leave: 16 weeks paid
   - Bereavement leave: 5 days

3. Benefits
   - Health insurance: Full coverage for employees and dependents
   - 401(k): Company match up to 6%
   - Stock options: Vesting over 4 years with 1-year cliff
   - Education stipend: $3,000/year for professional development

4. Performance Reviews
   Reviews are conducted semi-annually (June and December).
   Rating scale: 1 (Needs Improvement) to 5 (Exceptional)
   Bonus structure: 10-25% based on individual and company performance

5. Confidentiality
   All employees must sign an NDA upon hiring.
   Proprietary information includes source code, algorithms, customer lists,
   financial data, and strategic plans.
   Violations may result in immediate termination and legal action.

6. IT Security for Employees
   - Never share credentials via email or chat
   - Use company-approved password manager (1Password)
   - Report suspicious emails to phishing@quinine.ai
   - Lock workstation when leaving desk (Win+L or Cmd+Ctrl+Q)
   - Do not install unapproved software on company devices
