MERIDIAN SAVINGS ACCOUNT PRODUCT GUIDE

The Meridian Savings Account is designed for customers seeking secure long-term savings with competitive interest rates and easy account management.

Key Features:

* Interest Rate: 3.75% AER variable
* Minimum Opening Deposit: £100
* Monthly Maintenance Fee: £0
* Online and Mobile Banking Access
* Instant Internal Transfers
* FDIC-equivalent deposit protection under UK FSCS regulations

Withdrawal Policy:
Customers may perform unlimited withdrawals through online banking. Withdrawals exceeding £25,000 may require additional verification.

Account Eligibility:

* Must be at least 18 years old
* Valid government-issued identification
* Proof of address issued within the last 90 days

Fees:

* International Transfer Fee: £12
* Returned Payment Fee: £15
* Replacement Debit Card Fee: £8

For additional product information, customers should contact Meridian Customer Support or visit a local branch office.
