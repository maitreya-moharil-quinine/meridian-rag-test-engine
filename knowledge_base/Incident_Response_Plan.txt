MERIDIAN INCIDENT RESPONSE PLAN

Internal Use Only

Version: 2.5

Purpose:
This document defines the process for responding to cybersecurity incidents affecting Meridian Financial Services.

Incident Severity Levels:

Severity 1:
Confirmed compromise of customer information or critical business systems.

Severity 2:
Unauthorized access attempt, malware infection, or service disruption.

Severity 3:
Policy violations or suspicious activity requiring investigation.

Escalation Process:

1. Identify incident.
2. Contain affected systems.
3. Preserve evidence.
4. Perform investigation.
5. Remediate vulnerabilities.
6. Conduct post-incident review.

All suspected security incidents must be reported immediately to the Security Operations Team.
