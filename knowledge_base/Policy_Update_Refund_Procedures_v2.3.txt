POLICY UPDATE
REFUND PROCEDURES VERSION 2.3

Internal Use Only

Effective Date: March 2026

Summary of Changes

The Customer Operations Department has updated refund processing procedures to improve consistency and reduce resolution timelines.

Key Updates:

* Standard refund requests should be acknowledged within 24 hours.
* Fraud-related refund requests should receive priority review.
* Escalated complaints must be reviewed by a Support Supervisor before closure.
* Refund investigations exceeding 10 business days require management approval.

Documentation Requirements:

* Customer identification details
* Transaction reference number
* Supporting evidence where applicable
* Case management tracking identifier

Operational teams should ensure all refund decisions are documented within the Customer Case Management Platform.

Questions regarding this update should be directed to the Customer Operations Manager.
