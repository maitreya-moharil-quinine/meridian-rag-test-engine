MERIDIAN REFUND AND CANCELLATION POLICY

Version: 2.1
Effective Date: January 2026

Meridian Financial Services strives to ensure fair treatment of all customers regarding fees, service charges, and account-related disputes.

Eligible Refund Requests:

* Duplicate charges
* Unauthorized transactions
* Service fees charged in error
* Failed transfer fees

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the transaction date.

Customers may submit refund requests through:

* Online Banking Portal
* Customer Support Hotline
* Local Branch Office

Processing Timeline:

* Initial Review: 3 business days
* Investigation: Up to 10 business days
* Resolution Notification: Within 15 business days

Account Closure:
Customers may close their account at any time provided there are no outstanding obligations or pending investigations.

Meridian reserves the right to reject refund requests involving suspected fraud, policy abuse, or incomplete documentation.

Escalations may be submitted to the Customer Complaints Team if the customer is dissatisfied with the final outcome.
