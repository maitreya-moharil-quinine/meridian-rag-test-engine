KNOW YOUR CUSTOMER (KYC) VERIFICATION PROCESS

Internal Use Only

Meridian Financial Services performs identity verification for all new account applicants and existing customers requiring profile updates.

Required Documentation:

* Government-issued photo identification
* Proof of address
* Tax identification number
* Selfie verification image

Risk Classification Levels:
Low Risk:

* Standard retail customers

Medium Risk:

* Business account holders
* International customers

High Risk:

* Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)
* High-value account holders
* Customers from sanctioned jurisdictions

Verification Thresholds:
Accounts exceeding £50,000 in deposits within a 30-day period must undergo Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD).

Compliance Escalation Contact:
[compliance@meridian-finance.com](mailto:compliance@meridian-finance.com)

Failure to complete KYC verification may result in account restrictions or suspension.
