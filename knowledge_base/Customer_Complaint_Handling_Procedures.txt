CUSTOMER COMPLAINT HANDLING PROCEDURES

Internal Use Only

Purpose:
To ensure consistent and timely handling of customer complaints.

Complaint Categories:

* Transaction Disputes
* Account Access Issues
* Fraud Reports
* Service Quality Concerns
* Product Complaints

Example Customer Case

Customer ID: 100245
Customer Name: John Smith
Account Number: 44891234567
Complaint Type: Unauthorized Debit Transaction

Reported Transaction:
Date: 2026-02-12
Amount: £1,250.00
Merchant: Global Tech Services Ltd

Resolution:
Transaction investigated and determined to be fraudulent. Funds reimbursed within 7 business days.

Escalation Levels:
Level 1: Customer Service Representative
Level 2: Support Supervisor
Level 3: Customer Advocacy Team
Level 4: Compliance Department

All complaints must be logged within the Meridian Case Management System and assigned a tracking number.
