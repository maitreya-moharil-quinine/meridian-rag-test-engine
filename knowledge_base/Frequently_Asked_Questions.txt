MERIDIAN FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I check my account balance?
A: Customers can log in to the Meridian online banking portal or use the Meridian mobile application.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Select "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the verification instructions.

Q: How long do bank transfers take?
A: Internal Meridian transfers are processed immediately. External transfers generally complete within 1-3 business days.

Q: What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?
A: Contact Meridian Customer Support immediately at +44 20 7946 2200 to freeze the card and request a replacement.

Q: How can I dispute a transaction?
A: Submit a transaction dispute request through the customer portal or contact a support representative.

Q: Are my deposits protected?
A: Eligible deposits are protected under Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) regulations subject to applicable limits.

Q: How can I update my personal information?
A: Customers may update their address, phone number, and contact preferences through the online banking portal after identity verification.
