Quinine AI — Customer Support Guide

Refund Policy:
Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, a pro-rated refund is available for annual plans only. To request a refund, contact billing@quinine.ai with your account ID and reason.

Cancellation Process:
1. Log into your account at dashboard.quinine.ai
2. Navigate to Settings → Subscription
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period

Technical Support Tiers:
- Tier 1: Self-service knowledge base and community forums
- Tier 2: Email support (response within 24 hours)
- Tier 3: Priority support with dedicated engineer (Enterprise only)
- Tier 4: On-site support and custom SLA (Enterprise Plus only)

Common Troubleshooting:
1. API Key Issues: Ensure your key starts with "qai_" and has not expired
2. Rate Limiting: Default is 100 requests/minute. Contact sales for higher limits.
3. Model Timeout: Increase timeout in config.yaml, ensure stable network connection
4. Report Generation: Reports are stored in ./reports/ directory

Data Export:
Customers can export all their data in JSON or CSV format via the API:
  GET /api/v1/export?format=json
  Authorization: Bearer <your-api-key>

Account Deletion:
To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email privacy@quinine.ai from the registered email address. Deletion is processed within 14 business days per our GDPR obligations.
